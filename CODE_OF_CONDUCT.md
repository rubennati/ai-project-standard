# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We aim to maintain a respectful, professional, and inclusive collaboration environment.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and specific
- Assume good intent
- Focus on technical content and project goals
- Respect review processes and human decisions

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration workflows

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussions and contributions to protect project participants and project quality.
